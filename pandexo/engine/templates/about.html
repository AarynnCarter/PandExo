About PandExo

PandExo was written using the core capabilities of STScI's Pandeia (Pandeia + Exoplanets = PandExo). The front end of Pandexo is designed specifically for exoplanet transit studies. You might ask-- what's the difference between running Pandeia and PandExo? Pandeia requires that you know the number of groups, frames, and integrations. It also only takes in as input a stellar SED. PandExo takes in a stellar SED, a planet spectrum, and the transit duration and provides, as output, the error obtained on your spectrum. Other than that, the two are identical.
